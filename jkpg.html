    About Jönköping

    Jönköping


    Things you can do in Jönköping

    Sports

    If you like sports, you can for example go bowling. A newly famous sport called padel is very popular in Sweden and there's a lot of courts in Jönköping where you can play sports like padel, bowling, tennis, badminton, squash, climbing and curling. One of these places is called Racketcentrum in Rosenlund. You could also go swimming indoors at Rosenlunds Badhus or outdoors at Brunnstorp or in the lake Vättern (in the summer). If you like golf, there's some golf places in Jönköping. If you'd like, you could get tickets to see the local hockey team HV71 of Jönköping play at Husqvarna Garden (their home arena).



    Nature

    Sweden as you might know is known for its forests. There are a lot of nature preserve places you can visit and take a hike in, like Dumme Mosse, Bondberget and Vattenledningsparken. There are also a lot of parks, like Rådhusparken, Knektaparken, Rosenlunds Rosarium and Stadsparken. If you wanna visit a mine there is a mine called Tabergsgruvan in Taberg. There are also beautiful viewpoints in Jönköping like Vista kulle, Bondberget, Stadsparken and the mountain of Huskvarna.


    Gränna & Visingsö

    Gränna is an old beautiful town you can visit, located approximately 20 or 30 minutes away from Jönköping. It's mostly famous for the so called “polkagrisen” or "candy canes" which are hard minty candies with its original red and white stripes, which were invented in Gränna by Amalia Eriksson. You can also visit Amalia's home which is now a museum. Gränna is located by a harbor and you can take the ferry to the island Visingsö. Overall, it's a very humble small town with a lot of small shops selling polkagrisar and ice cream. It's a very popular location for tourists.


    Events

    If you want to go to concerts there is a park called Folkets park where they sometimes have artist play. Elmia is a place that holds a lot of events. If you like gaming, you could visit Dreamhack at Elmia which takes place now and then. If you like cars, then there's a lot of car shows at Elmia too.


    Good places to go out

    If you're looking for pubs, bars or partying, you could visit Harrys, O'Learys, Sliver, Moore or Seaside. If you're looking for shopping, the place to go is the shopping center Asecs or “Stan”. If you like books, you should visit Akademibokhandeln.


    Good places to eat

    The best pizza place is called Biblos, located in Huskvarna. There are many good restaurants at the harbor pier, “hamnpiren” in Jönköping, which is overall also a nice-looking place. If you like Sushi, you could try Sushi Heroes. If you like Asian food, restaurant Basilika might be your option. If you like middle eastern food, you could try Samboosak or Mr. BBQ. La Vue is also a popular restaurant. On a hot summer day, you might want to get an ice cream at Glassfabriken. Looking for candy/snacks? Your option would be Go Banana or Hemmakväll.
